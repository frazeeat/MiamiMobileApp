About
Located in the center of campus, Miami’s Armstrong Student Center provides quick and convenient access to a wide variety of cuisines. This location features many on-campus dining options including Haines’ Boulangerie, Mein Street Mongolian & Asian Grill, Wing Bar, Pulley Diner, Sundial Pizza Company, Miami Ice, Emporium, and new for fall 2016, Miami Traditions featuring carved meats, roasted chicken, and a variety of freshly made sides. Select options at this location are open 24 hours daily during the school year.
